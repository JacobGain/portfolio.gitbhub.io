My Work Experience

Here is a little bit about my previous work experience!

Michelis Spray Foam Insulation Inc.

Full-time summer job, May 2023-August 2023

In the summer after my first year of University, I started looking for a job. Through a mutual connection, I got set up at Michelis Spray Foam Insulation Inc. as a labourer. My role was small in the beginning, learning from my coworkers and helping others without much of my own responsibility. As the summer went on and I got the hang of everything, I took on more responsibility. I started driving the trucks and was able to go to jobs with just myself and the sprayer, as opposed to crews of 3 or 4. I learned a lot during my time at Michelis Spray Foam, including but not limited to the importance of punctuality, taking accountability, and working as a team.

Pictured above is one of the trucks I got to drive!


Trevor Gain & Associates

Part-time, January 2019-September 2022

My father, Trevor Gain, is an architect who owns his own business. During the time of the COVID-19 pandemic, all of southern Ontario's residents were stuck in their homes. This made homeowners realize that they wanted to reconstruct or renovate their homes. This meant my dad needed more help if he was to keep up with the increasing demand for his services. He employed my brother and I as administrative assistants to help with the smaller things, meaning he could continue working on the more important designs. We most often did his filing, prepared his projects for the Committee of Adjustment, and analyzed housing data. The most important lessons I took away from this job were maintaining organization and the importance of having required tasks done on time.
